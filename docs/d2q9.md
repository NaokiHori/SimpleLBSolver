# Metrics

We consider D2Q9 model throughout this project.
The nine velocity vectors (in lattice unit) and weights are given as follows:

```math
\begin{aligned}
    \vec{c}_0 &=   0 \vec{e}_x + 0 \vec{e}_y, \\
    \vec{c}_1 &=   1 \vec{e}_x + 0 \vec{e}_y, \\
    \vec{c}_2 &=   0 \vec{e}_x + 1 \vec{e}_y, \\
    \vec{c}_3 &= - 1 \vec{e}_x + 0 \vec{e}_y, \\
    \vec{c}_4 &=   0 \vec{e}_x - 1 \vec{e}_y, \\
    \vec{c}_5 &=   1 \vec{e}_x + 1 \vec{e}_y, \\
    \vec{c}_6 &= - 1 \vec{e}_x + 1 \vec{e}_y, \\
    \vec{c}_7 &= - 1 \vec{e}_x - 1 \vec{e}_y, \\
    \vec{c}_8 &=   1 \vec{e}_x - 1 \vec{e}_y,
\end{aligned}
```

```math
\begin{aligned}
    W_0 &= w_0, \\
    W_1 &= w_1, w_2, w_3, w_4, \\
    W_2 &= w_5, w_6, w_7, w_8,
\end{aligned}
```

with $W_0 \equiv 16 / 36$, $W_1 \equiv 4 / 36$, $W_2 \equiv 1 / 36$.

Note that the speed of sound $c_s$ is $1 / \sqrt{3}$.
